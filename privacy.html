App Permissions Explained

We request only the permissions needed for features you actually use.

📷
Camera
iOS & Android Used to let you take photos or scan documents within the app. We never access the camera in the background. Photos are processed on-device and not uploaded without your action. iOS: NSCameraUsageDescription | Android: CAMERA
🖼️
Photo Library
iOS & Android Lets you select photos from your library to use in-app. On iOS 14+, we use the limited photo picker — you choose exactly which photos to share. We never read your full photo library without permission. iOS: NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription | Android: READ_MEDIA_IMAGES
📍
Location
iOS & Android Requested only in apps where location is core to the feature (e.g. maps, local search). We always use "When In Use" unless "Always" is explicitly required and disclosed. Precise location is requested only when approximate is insufficient. iOS: NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription | Android: ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / COARSE_LOCATION
🎤
Microphone
iOS & Android Only used in apps with voice recording or audio features. We never record audio in the background. Audio data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it. iOS: NSMicrophoneUsageDescription | Android: RECORD_AUDIO
🔔
Push Notifications
iOS & Android We ask before sending any notifications. Transactional notifications (e.g. order updates) may be enabled by default but you can turn them off at any time in device Settings. Marketing notifications require a separate opt-in. iOS: UNUserNotificationCenter | Android: POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)
👤
Contacts
iOS & Android Only requested in apps with contact-sharing features. We never upload your entire contact list to our servers. On iOS, we use the CNContact framework with minimum necessary access. iOS: NSContactsUsageDescription | Android: READ_CONTACTS
🩺
Health & Fitness Data
iOS Only Health data accessed only via Apple HealthKit in health/fitness apps. Requires explicit HealthKit authorization. Health data is never used for advertising, never sold, and never shared with third parties without separate written consent. iOS: NSHealthShareUsageDescription, NSHealthUpdateUsageDescription
📦
Storage / Files
Android Only READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE used only to save or access files the user explicitly creates with the app. Scoped Storage (Android 10+) enforced — we don't read files outside our app's folder without using the system file picker. Android: READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (where applicable)

Tracking & Advertising

Apple ATT (App Tracking Transparency): Under iOS 14.5 and later, we are legally required — and willingly choose — to ask your permission before tracking you across apps and websites owned by other companies. If you decline, we do not track you and you receive contextual (non-personalized) ads instead of behavioral ones.

In some free apps, we show ads powered by Google AdMob. Here's how it works:

  • With your consent (ATT / GDPR consent granted): Personalized ads may be shown using your Advertising ID. You may see ads relevant to your interests.
  • Without consent: Non-personalized (contextual) ads only. No tracking. No profile building.
  • You can reset or limit ad tracking at any time: iOS → Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking | Android → Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads across all Google services at adssettings.google.com.

In-App Purchases & Payments

We never see or store your payment card details. All payments are processed exclusively by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). AppChunks only receives a transaction confirmation and subscription status.

Subscriptions (iOS — StoreKit): Auto-renewable subscriptions on iOS are governed by Apple's standard subscription terms. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel subscriptions at: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

Subscriptions (Android — Google Play Billing): Subscriptions are managed by Google Play. Cancel at: Google Play Store → Subscriptions.

Refunds: For refund requests on iOS, contact Apple Support at reportaproblem.apple.com. For Android, contact Google Play Support. AppChunks does not process refunds directly for App Store or Play Store purchases.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

COPPA Compliance: We comply fully with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations globally (EU GDPR Article 8, UK GDPR, etc.).

Our general-audience apps are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

Apps in the Kids Category (Apple) or Designed for Families (Google Play):

  • No behavioral or interest-based advertising
  • No third-party analytics SDKs except those approved by Apple/Google for children's apps
  • No social media links or account sharing without a parental gate
  • No collection of personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for the app to function
  • Parental consent obtained before any optional data collection
  • Content rated appropriate for the age group
  • No links to external websites without a parental gate

Parents may contact us at privacy@appchunks.com to request review or deletion of any data we may have inadvertently collected from their child.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Hashed passwords using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Access controls — minimum necessary access for all team members
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning
  • App Transport Security (ATS) enforced on iOS — no cleartext HTTP
  • Certificate pinning in sensitive apps
  • OWASP Mobile Top 10 guidelines followed during development
No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law (within 72 hours for GDPR-relevant breaches).
